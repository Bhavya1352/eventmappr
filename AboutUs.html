Our Mission

At EventMappr, we believe that vibrant communities are built through meaningful local connections. Our mission is to make discovering and sharing local events as intuitive and accessible as possible.

We created EventMappr to solve a simple problem: finding what's happening around you shouldn't be complicated. By visualizing events on an interactive map, we've made it easier than ever to see what's happening in your neighborhood.

Community First

We prioritize building tools that strengthen local connections and empower community organizers.

Accessibility

We're committed to making our platform accessible to everyone, regardless of technical ability.

Trust & Safety

We maintain high standards for event listings and protect user privacy and data.
